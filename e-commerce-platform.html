From August to December 2021, I worked as a UI/UX Designer at Momoisland, an e-commerce platform specializing in the sale of cosmetics, daily essentials, and snacks from Southeast Asia.

My Role
Product Design and marketing design, Aug 1, 2021 to Dec 31, 2021
Introduction
Back in May 2021, the company outsourced the development of the app to a design studio in Toronto. By October, disagreements arose between the company and the app development team regarding the design, leading to the termination of the collaboration during the design phase. In November, I started redesigning the UI/UX of the app.
At that time, I had been working at the company for 2 months and had completed 2 websites, one of which was an e-commerce site built by Shopify. This experience made me very familiar with the company's products.
Logo
Brand Color
The main color theme for Momo's logo is orange-pink, complemented by a low-saturation coffee-colored font. This design choice is inspired by the brand founder's preference for a cute and vibrant girlish style.
‍
Goals
• Feeling : elegant & cartoonish
• Warm colour palette
• Usability comes first
• Target customers:  age18-35 Asian female oriented in North America
• Want users to think of good quality, premium, young, dynamic
• Now mainly focus on food/cosmetic product
• Will move toward healthy product in the future
• Associated with Gaming in the future
Framwork
The homepage content primarily includes category navigation for products, recent promotions, special offers, featured products, and new arrivals.
The category page is divided into functional categories, regional categories, and brand categories.
Wireframes
Find UI
From login to the homepage, click to enter a product page, add to the shopping cart, proceed to payment, and view the orders.
Summary
For this project, I had only one and a half months to complete the UX/UI design. Fortunately, I had already been with the company for two months and successfully developed two websites. This experience gave me a thorough understanding of the company’s philosophy, product architecture, and target audience. It proved to be invaluable, allowing me to quickly adapt to the role and complete the project on time.
